# Description

Lack of support from employees or the wider organization due to fear of change or attachment to the old system.

# Root Cause

Cultural entrenchment in legacy processes and lack of clear communication about modernization benefits.

# Trigger Event

Introduction of the modernization project or new software interfaces.

# Impact

Project sabotage, slow adoption, failure to realize business benefits.

# Linked Risks

- Funding withdrawal
- Project cancellation
- Time Overrun

# Stakeholders

- Employees
- IT Staff
- Management

# Contextual Constraints

- Trust in legacy system


# Detection Source

Staff Surveys

# Standardized Risk Entry

Modernization efforts often face internal resistance due to a fear of expertise obsolescence and a cultural preference for "proven" reliable legacy systems.

# Key Quote for Prompting

> "Resistance to change from the organization... they fear that their expertise and professional experience... may become redundant."

# Logic Pattern

IF [Shift to New Technology] AND [lack of communication] THEN [Cultural and Staff Resistance]
