# Description
For each risk, document mitigation actions, responsible parties, resolution timelines, and status.

# Prerequisites
Risks have been identified in a risk register.

# Tools / Technologies
Risk register; mitigation tracking; project plan

# Trade-offs / Side Effects


# Effectiveness
Ensures risks are actively managed and resolved.

# Stakeholders
- Risk owners
- responsible parties
- governance team
- migration team

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies to identified technical
- operational
- and organizational risks.


# Standardized Mitigation Entry
Tracking mitigation actions, owners, timelines, and status operationalizes the relationship between modernization risks and their mitigations.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "This tracking ensures that you actively manage and resolve risks"

# Logic Pattern
IF a risk is recorded THEN APPLY mitigation action, owner, timeline, and status tracking.
