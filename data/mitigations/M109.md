# Standardized Mitigation Entry
Output-review requirements mitigate AI governance and audit risk by recording prompts, reviewers, and validation evidence. They create the audit trail required for controlled AI-assisted modernization.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "Output-review requirements — standards documenting the prompt used, the reviewer who approved the output, and the test evidence validating its correctness."
