# Description
Replace outdated business process tooling with modern collaboration, workflow automation, and low-code platforms to streamline processes and accelerate development/deployment.

# Prerequisites
Frontline or line-of-business applications with business process workflows.

# Tools / Technologies
Microsoft Copilot Studio; Microsoft Power Platform; Microsoft Teams; Azure Logic Apps; low-code solutions

# Trade-offs / Side Effects


# Effectiveness
The guidance states that low-code solutions can accelerate development and deployment and that automated low-code workflows support rapid prototyping.

# Stakeholders
- Internal stakeholders
- business process owners
- developers
- application owners

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies to frontline applications and complex code-heavy line-of-business processes.
