# Description
OMB should provide guidance requiring agencies to develop prioritized legacy-system inventories, IT modernization plans, and supporting budgets.

# Prerequisites
OMB authority to issue guidance; agency ability to inventory systems and prepare plans and budgets.

# Tools / Technologies
Technology Modernization Fund; working capital funds

# Trade-offs / Side Effects


# Effectiveness


# Stakeholders
- OMB; federal agencies; agency CIOs; Congress

# Contextual Constraints

- Federal agencies modernizing portfolios of legacy IT systems.


# Standardized Mitigation Entry
Require agencies to create prioritized inventories, modernization plans, and supporting budgets so modernization work is planned and funded before execution.

> Paraphrased evidence: OMB should require inventories, modernization plans, and budgets.
