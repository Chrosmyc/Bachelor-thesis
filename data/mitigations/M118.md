# Description
Transition to Agile DevOps processes for maintaining legacy applications during modernization.

# Prerequisites
Teams capable of adopting Agile DevOps practices; applications under modernization or maintenance

# Tools / Technologies
Agile DevOps processes

# Trade-offs / Side Effects


# Effectiveness
Presented as a way to deliver applications with mission-required speed and listed as a government best practice.

# Stakeholders
- DevOps teams; developers; operations teams; modernization team; mission owners

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies to legacy applications and systems during modernization and ongoing maintenance.


# Standardized Mitigation Entry
Transitioning to Agile DevOps mitigates modernization momentum and delivery risks by improving the speed and adaptability of application delivery and maintenance.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> Paraphrased evidence: Agile DevOps helps deliver at mission-required speed.

# Logic Pattern
IF modernization requires faster and adaptive delivery THEN APPLY Agile DevOps processes to application maintenance and modernization.
