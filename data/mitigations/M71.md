# Description
Identify limitations, expected benefits, business goals, available resources, feasibility, and expected modernization duration before starting the migration.

# Prerequisites
Driving forces, business goals, resources, and stakeholder input are available.

# Tools / Technologies


# Trade-offs / Side Effects
Requires stakeholder alignment and upfront analysis; trade-offs may reveal conflicting goals.

# Effectiveness
The paper states this activity is paramount to avoid unsuccessful modernization and waste of resources.

# Stakeholders
- Managers; engineers; developers; customers

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies in initiation before deciding whether to modernize with microservices.


# Standardized Mitigation Entry
Analyze driving forces and feasibility before modernization to reduce the risk of unsuccessful migration and wasted resources.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> Analyze the driving forces is paramount to avoid unsuccessful modernization and waste of resources.

# Logic Pattern
IF modernization feasibility is uncertain THEN APPLY driving-force and feasibility analysis.
