# Description
Determine how critical the data is that is processed, moved, and stored; prioritize data importance from high to moderate to low so the team understands the significance of each data category.

# Prerequisites
Knowledge of the data being processed, moved, and stored.

# Trade-offs / Side Effects


# Effectiveness


# Stakeholders
- Modernization team; data owners; business stakeholders

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies when modernization includes data processing
- movement
- storage
- or migration.


# Standardized Mitigation Entry
Assess and rank modernization data by criticality so teams can focus attention on high-importance data during processing, movement, and storage.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "Prioritize the importance of the data from high to moderate to low."

# Logic Pattern
IF data is processed, moved, or stored during modernization THEN evaluate its criticality and prioritize it from high to moderate to low.
