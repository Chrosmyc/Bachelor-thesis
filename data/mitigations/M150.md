# Description
OMB should establish a program under the federal CIO, similar to the United States Digital Service, involving public-private partnership with technology providers. Agencies not making enough progress could seek assistance converting, re-engineering, or redesigning legacy applications.

# Prerequisites
Federal CIO program; technology industry participation; agency request for assistance.

# Tools / Technologies
Current-century hardware and software platforms; cloud and big data technologies where applicable

# Trade-offs / Side Effects


# Effectiveness


# Stakeholders
- OMB; federal CIO; agencies; technology industry providers

# Contextual Constraints

- Agencies struggling to convert legacy applications or needing specialized modernization expertise.


# Standardized Mitigation Entry
Provide expert public-private assistance to agencies struggling with conversion so older systems can be redesigned or re-engineered smoothly without disrupting mission operations.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> Paraphrased evidence: the paper recommends a public-private program for agencies not making enough conversion progress.

# Logic Pattern
IF an agency lacks progress converting legacy applications THEN PROVIDE expert public-private modernization assistance.
