# Description
Shift legacy workloads to modern commodity platforms with as little change as possible. The paper presents rehost as the lowest-risk, least-cost, fastest time-to-value alternative and as the most realistic path for government modernization.

# Prerequisites
Existing workloads that can be shifted to commodity platforms; target commodity platform availability; modernization plan

# Tools / Technologies
Commodity platforms

# Trade-offs / Side Effects


# Effectiveness
Reported as having repeatable success, predictable budget and schedule performance, cost savings, lower risk, fastest implementation, and faster time-to-value.

# Stakeholders
- Modernization team; platform teams; program leadership; budget owners; mission owners

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies when modernization can initially preserve workload behavior while moving to modern commodity platforms.


# Standardized Mitigation Entry
Rehosting mitigates high-change modernization risk by moving workloads to commodity platforms with minimal change, reducing cost, schedule, and delivery uncertainty compared with replace or rewrite.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> Paraphrased evidence: rehost is presented as lowest risk and fastest time-to-value.

# Logic Pattern
IF modernization risk is driven by high-change replacement or rewrite THEN APPLY rehost with minimal change to reduce delivery and cost risk.
