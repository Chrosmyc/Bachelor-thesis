# Description
Provide partner teams with guidance about enterprise constraints, needs, portfolio context, and ways of working.

# Prerequisites
Internal teams understand constraints and communicate them to partner teams

# Tools / Technologies
Partner guidance; knowledge transfer

# Trade-offs / Side Effects


# Effectiveness
Directly recommended to avoid unusable partner-built software and excessive partner learning time.

# Stakeholders
- Internal teams
- external partners
- compliance stakeholders
- operations
- architects

# Contextual Constraints

- Applicable when partners lack domain or enterprise context


# Standardized Mitigation Entry
Guiding partners on constraints and needs reduces the risk that they build unusable software or spend excessive time learning the portfolio.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "provide guidance to partner teams"

# Logic Pattern
IF partners lack enterprise context THEN guide them on constraints, needs, portfolio, and working practices.
