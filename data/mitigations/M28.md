# Description
Organizations should balance legacy system stability with long-term transformation goals and align modernization with organizational objectives, risk tolerance, and operational continuity requirements.

# Prerequisites
Clear organizational objectives; defined risk tolerance; continuity requirements; governance oversight

# Tools / Technologies
COBIT; ISACA governance models

# Trade-offs / Side Effects


# Effectiveness
Helps avoid operational risk from aggressive automation and risks from avoiding modernization

# Stakeholders
- Leadership; governance teams; technical teams; operational teams

# Contextual Constraints

- Organizations deciding how aggressively to automate or modernize


# Standardized Mitigation Entry
A balanced governance approach aligns modernization with business objectives, risk tolerance, and operational continuity, reducing risks from both aggressive automation and avoidance.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "A balanced governance approach is therefore essential."

# Logic Pattern
IF modernization decisions involve trade-offs between stability and transformation THEN APPLY balanced governance aligned with objectives, risk tolerance, and continuity.
