# Secondary Risk Created
Requires additional documentation and maintenance of component information.

# Applicability Conditions
Target architecture with multiple logical components and functional dependencies.

# Observed Outcome
A business component catalogue captures reusability and dependencies, supporting safer sequencing and parallelization of modernization work.

# Reasoning Notes
Business component catalogue and dependency overview (M83) targets the root cause of Migration Reversal Risk (R95): Adoption of architectures without sufficient evaluation.

# Standardized Relationship Entry
IF Create a catalogue for each business component showing reusability and functional dependencies with other components, enabling independent and parallel migration planning THEN the risk of migration reversal risk (R95) is prevented.
