# Applicability Conditions
Applies when modernization can initially preserve workload behavior while moving to modern commodity platforms.

# Observed Outcome
Rehosting mitigates high-change modernization risk by moving workloads to commodity platforms with minimal change, reducing cost, schedule, and delivery uncertainty compared with replace or rewrite.

# Reasoning Notes
Rehost with Minimal Change (M116) targets the root cause of Unknown Legacy Behavior During Modernization (R119): Incomplete understanding of legacy application behavior, dependencies, and implementation details.

# Standardized Relationship Entry
IF Shift legacy workloads to modern commodity platforms with as little change as possible. The paper presents rehost as the lowest-risk, least-cost, fastest time-to-value alternative and as the most realistic path for government modernization THEN the risk of unknown legacy behavior during modernization (R119) is prevented.
