# Secondary Risk Created
Modernizing during migration can increase complexity and timeline risk.

# Applicability Conditions
The workload is problematic and would otherwise be rehosted even though modernization work is likely or needed soon.

# Observed Outcome
Reduces the likelihood that initial rehost work becomes temporary work that must be redone later.

# Reasoning Notes
The relationship is indirect but grounded in G10's warning that problematic workloads should not be rehosted unchanged and that modernization should address root causes during migration.

# Standardized Relationship Entry
M125 reduces R125 by replacing short-lived plain rehost with modernization during migration when root-cause work is already necessary.
