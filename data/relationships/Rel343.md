# Applicability Conditions
Systems with cross-system business workflows.

# Observed Outcome
When Business process and dependency validation risk (R46) occurs, it can cascade into Premature or Unsafe Legacy System Retirement (R52) because Access to the legacy system is discontinued too early or without safeguards.

# Reasoning Notes
Business process and dependency validation risk (R46) produces the consequence(s) 'Post-implementation defects, workflow breakage, data inconsistency, and user experience issues.', which aligns with the root cause of Premature or Unsafe Legacy System Retirement (R52): Abrupt discontinuation; lack of training; missing backup for future reference..

# Standardized Relationship Entry
IF Business process and dependency validation risk (R46) materializes THEN it can trigger or worsen Premature or Unsafe Legacy System Retirement (R52).
