# Applicability Conditions
Breaking down a monolithic legacy system into heavily decoupled microservices without a strong SOA governance framework.

# Observed Outcome
The number of services explodes, leading to tracing difficulties and unmanageable complexity.

# Reasoning Notes
Component decoupling inherently increases the number of moving parts, networking layers, and APIs, triggering an orchestration crisis if left unmanaged.

# Standardized Relationship Entry
IF component decoupling is aggressively applied THEN the risk of orchestration and complexity explosion is introduced.
