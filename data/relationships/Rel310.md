# Secondary Risk Created
Requires time and effort from scarce experts.

# Applicability Conditions
Legacy systems with scarce skills, ageing experts, staff turnover, and incomplete documentation.

# Observed Outcome
Knowledge harvesting preserves scarce legacy expertise and improves migration decisions by capturing interviews, training knowledge, and updating documentation.

# Reasoning Notes
Knowledge harvesting and documentation update (M88) targets the root cause of Knowledge erosion during migration (R94): Critical knowledge resides in people and documentation is incomplete or outdated.

# Standardized Relationship Entry
IF Conduct and archive interviews, initiate knowledge transfer programs, organize focused training, and update or create documentation to preserve existing system knowledge THEN the risk of knowledge erosion during migration (R94) is prevented.
