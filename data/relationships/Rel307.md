# Observed Outcome
Controlled outsourcing mitigates focus and cost risks by limiting external development to low-priority features under strict strategic guidelines.

# Reasoning Notes
Controlled outsourcing for low-priority features (M87) targets the root cause of Unclear scope and cross-system dependency risk (R99): Current environment, interfaces, dependencies, and complexity are not fully understood.

# Standardized Relationship Entry
