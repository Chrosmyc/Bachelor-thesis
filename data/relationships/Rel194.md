# Secondary Risk Created


# Applicability Conditions
Applies when a rebuild initiative is considered but organizational readiness, agility, or maturity is insufficient.

# Observed Outcome
The guidance recommends retaining the application until readiness barriers are resolved rather than proceeding with modernization prematurely.

# Reasoning Notes
The paper explicitly states that retain is the appropriate strategy when modernization readiness is limited by cost, dependencies, risks, or other factors.

# Standardized Relationship Entry
Retaining the application until organizational readiness improves reduces the risk that a rebuild initiative is undertaken without sufficient business agility or organizational maturity.
