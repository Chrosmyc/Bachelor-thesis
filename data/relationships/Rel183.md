# Secondary Risk Created


# Applicability Conditions
Applies when the main alternative is COTS replacement or rip-and-replace and functional coverage gaps are likely.

# Observed Outcome
The paper presents rehost as the lower-risk alternative to replacement approaches.

# Reasoning Notes
Rehost reduces reliance on a COTS package matching all legacy functionality because workloads are shifted with minimal change rather than replaced wholesale.

# Standardized Relationship Entry
Rehosting reduces COTS functional coverage-gap risk by avoiding a high-change replacement and preserving workload behavior as much as possible.
