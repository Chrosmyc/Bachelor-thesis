# Applicability Conditions
Replacing custom legacy software with standard COTS (Commercial Off-The-Shelf) products.

# Observed Outcome
The organization becomes dependent on the vendor's roadmap and is subject to forced upgrades and license fee hikes.

# Reasoning Notes
Transitioning to standardized products intrinsically shifts control from internal IT to an external vendor, exposing the business to licensing and upgrade dependencies.

# Standardized Relationship Entry
IF transition to standard software products is selected THEN COTS vendor upgrade and license fee dependency risks are introduced.
