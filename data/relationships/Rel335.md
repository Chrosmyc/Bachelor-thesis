# Secondary Risk Created
Modernization investment may seem expensive; budget constraints and operational stability are mentioned as reasons for postponement

# Applicability Conditions
Applies when modernization is being postponed or ignored despite business, operational, or security risks

# Observed Outcome
On-time modernization mitigates the broad risk set created by delayed modernization by reducing reliance on outdated systems and enabling efficiency, agility, security, and growth.

# Reasoning Notes
On-time System Modernization (M161) targets the root cause of Increased Opportunity Cost and Reduced Innovation Capacity (R156): Lack of flexibility to integrate new technologies or adapt to market demands

# Standardized Relationship Entry
IF Avoid postponing modernization and modernize systems before the cost of inaction becomes larger than the investment. The paper frames modernization as the central response to profit, productivity, opportunity, customer, security, and maintenance-cost risks THEN the risk of increased opportunity cost and reduced innovation capacity (R156) is prevented.
