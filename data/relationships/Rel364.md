# Observed Outcome
When SAP modernization path does not fit mission-critical workload requirements (R117) occurs, it can cascade into Partner-Built Software Cannot Be Used (R120) because External partners lead or execute modernization without internal guidance.

# Reasoning Notes
SAP modernization path does not fit mission-critical workload requirements (R117) produces the consequence(s) 'Modernization may not cut costs, increase agility, or deliver transformational business value', which aligns with the root cause of Partner-Built Software Cannot Be Used (R120): Missing internal ownership, missing domain knowledge, and insufficient communication of enterprise constraints.
