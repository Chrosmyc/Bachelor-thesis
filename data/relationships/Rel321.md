# Observed Outcome
Rehosting mitigates high-change modernization risk by moving workloads to commodity platforms with minimal change, reducing cost, schedule, and delivery uncertainty compared with replace or rewrite.

# Reasoning Notes
Rehost with Minimal Change (M116) targets the root cause of COTS Functional Coverage Gap (R118): Decades of legacy-system modification created customized functionality that cannot simply be swapped out.

# Standardized Relationship Entry

The paper presents rehost as the lowest-risk, least-cost, fastest time-to-value alternative and as the most realistic path for government modernization THEN the risk of cots functional coverage gap (R118) is prevented.
