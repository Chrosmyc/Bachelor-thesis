# Secondary Risk Created


# Applicability Conditions
Applies when replacement or rewrite would require full reproduction of poorly understood legacy behavior and a rehost option is feasible.

# Observed Outcome
The paper presents rehost as shifting workloads to commodity platforms with as little change as possible and as lower risk, lower cost, faster to implement, and faster to value than replacement or rewrite alternatives.

# Reasoning Notes
Rehost does not fully discover unknown legacy behavior by itself, but it reduces exposure to unknown requirements by minimizing change and avoiding a full replacement or rewrite that must recreate all hidden functionality.

# Standardized Relationship Entry
Rehosting reduces unknown-legacy-behavior risk by minimizing change and avoiding a full replacement or rewrite that must rediscover and recreate hidden functionality.
