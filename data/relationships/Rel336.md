On-time modernization mitigates the broad risk set created by delayed modernization by reducing reliance on outdated systems and enabling efficiency, agility, security, and growth.

# Reasoning Notes
On-time System Modernization (M161) targets the root cause of Customer Exit Due to Delayed Modernization (R157): Outdated systems cannot keep pace with evolving customer expectations and create a disjointed experience
