# Applicability Conditions
The Strangler Pattern is chosen for a massive, mission-critical monolith.

# Observed Outcome
The organization is forced to manage, monitor, and fund two parallel environments for years.

# Reasoning Notes
By definition, the Strangler Pattern (gradual replacement) requires the legacy system and the new system to coexist, inherently creating a prolonged dual-system transition.

# Standardized Relationship Entry
IF the Strangler Pattern is utilized THEN the risk of a prolonged dual-system transition is introduced.
