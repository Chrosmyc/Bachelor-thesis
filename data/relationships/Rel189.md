# Secondary Risk Created
Modernizing during migration can increase complexity and timeline risk.

# Applicability Conditions
The workload has unresolved performance, reliability, architectural, or technical debt problems and rehost is being considered.

# Observed Outcome
Avoids carrying unresolved issues and technical debt into Azure and reduces later rework.

# Reasoning Notes
G10 explicitly states that rehosting does not resolve existing performance, reliability, or architectural issues and recommends modernizing problematic workloads during migration to address root causes.

# Standardized Relationship Entry
M125 prevents R124 by avoiding plain rehost for problematic workloads and modernizing root causes before unresolved issues are carried into Azure.
