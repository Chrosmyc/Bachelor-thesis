# Description
Rebuilding can be risky when the organization lacks the high business agility and organizational maturity required for full innovation and optimization.

# Root Cause
Rebuilding is associated with high business agility, organizational maturity, and later modernization cycles.

# Trigger Event
Starting a rebuild initiative without sufficient agility or organizational maturity.

# Impact


# Linked Risks

# Stakeholders

- Business leadership
- modernization team
- architects
- operations teams

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies to rebuild initiatives
- especially critical solutions using cloud-native platforms
- advanced technologies
- and automation.


# Detection Source


# Standardized Risk Entry
A rebuild initiative may become risky if the organization lacks the agility and maturity that the guidance treats as necessary for full modernization and optimization.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> Source cue: rebuilding requires high business agility and organizational maturity.

# Logic Pattern
IF rebuild is initiated AND business agility or organizational maturity is insufficient THEN rebuild maturity risk may occur.
