# Description
Balancing people between legacy support and new-system development may become complex, especially when both old and new technologies require skills.

# Root Cause
Modernization requires simultaneous maintenance of the old system and development of the new one.

# Trigger Event
Teams are split across legacy operations, migration work, and new system build-out.

# Impact
Capacity bottlenecks, slow delivery, training needs, uneven support, and coordination overhead.

# Linked Risks
- R137; R146; R151

# Stakeholders

- Engineering teams; project managers; leadership; operations

# Contextual Constraints

- Organizations with limited staff or limited skills across both old and new technologies.


# Detection Source
Project Plan / Team Plan

# Standardized Risk Entry
If modernization requires simultaneous legacy support and new-system development, people allocation and skill gaps can slow or complicate the transition.

> 

# Logic Pattern
IF teams must support old technology AND build new technology THEN people allocation risk may materialize.
