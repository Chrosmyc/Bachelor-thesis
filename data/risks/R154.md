# Description
If containers are chosen for modernization, the paper states that container orchestrators or container management software are required to meet the modernization goal.

# Root Cause
Containers require orchestration or management software to meet modernization goals.

# Trigger Event
Choosing containers as a means of modernizing legacy software without suitable container orchestration or management software.

# Impact
The container-based modernization goal may not be met.

# Linked Risks

# Stakeholders

- Modernization team; operations; platform team

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies when containers are selected as the modernization approach.


# Detection Source
Architecture diagram / project plan

# Standardized Risk Entry
Container-based modernization may fail to meet its goal if container orchestration or management software is missing.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "If a company chooses to use containers as a means of modernizing its legacy software, it will require container orchestrators or container management software to meet its goal."

# Logic Pattern
IF containers are selected for modernization AND no container orchestration or management software is used THEN the modernization goal may not be met.
