# Description
Previous failed projects, surprise changes, and broken promises can create cynicism that undermines belief in modernization initiatives.

# Root Cause
Accumulated emotional debt from prior technology change failures and broken promises.

# Trigger Event
Leadership announces a new modernization push in an environment with low trust.

# Impact
Hidden skepticism; reduced commitment; risks remain unspoken; unrealistic roadmap.

# Linked Risks

# Stakeholders

- Employees; team leads; CIO; leadership; change management teams

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies where teams have experienced repeated failed modernization or technology change initiatives.


# Detection Source
Interview / Retrospective / Workshop

# Standardized Risk Entry
Modernization can be undermined when emotional debt creates quiet cynicism and prevents teams from openly surfacing risks.

> 

# Logic Pattern
IF previous change initiatives damaged trust AND a new modernization push begins THEN hidden resistance and unspoken risk increase.
