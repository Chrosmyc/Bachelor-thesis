# Description
Modernization may be hindered if teams lack training or development culture such as unit testing practices.

# Root Cause
Missing team preparation, organizational culture, and training.

# Trigger Event
Microservice development begins without aligning teams and practices.

# Impact
Development and modernization practices may be inconsistent or ineffective.

# Linked Risks

# Stakeholders

- Development teams
- organization
- project managers

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies during planning and preparation for MSA modernization.


# Detection Source
Project Plan / Interview

# Standardized Risk Entry
Teams that are not trained or aligned around development practices may struggle to execute microservice modernization effectively.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "training can be an effective measure to align the interests of organizations with development practices"

# Logic Pattern
IF teams lack training and development culture THEN modernization execution risk increases.
