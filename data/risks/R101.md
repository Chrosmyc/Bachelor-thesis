# Description
Deployment can be smooth or fraught with mistakes depending on the quality and execution of deployment plans; the middle of transition is when problems are most likely.

# Root Cause
Deployment involves complex, interdependent, concurrent activities across people, processes, infrastructure, data, operations, and support functions.

# Trigger Event
New system deployment or production cutover.

# Impact
Dissatisfied users, serious business disruption, failed transition.

# Linked Risks
- R102

# Stakeholders

- End users; operations; IT; finance; contracting; business owners

# Contextual Constraints

- Production deployment and transition periods.


# Detection Source
Project Plan

# Standardized Risk Entry
Deployment is a high-risk transition point where poor planning can cause mistakes, dissatisfied users, and serious disruption to business.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "If anything is going to go wrong, the middle of a transition is when it is most likely to happen."

# Logic Pattern
IF deployment activities are complex and not carefully orchestrated THEN transition failure and business disruption may occur.
