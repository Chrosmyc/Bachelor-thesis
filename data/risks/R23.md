# Description
Modernization efforts may fail to satisfy evolving regulatory and governance requirements.

# Root Cause
Legacy systems designed before modern compliance standards

# Trigger Event
Regulatory audits or policy updates

# Impact
Financial penalties, legal exposure, reputational damage

# Linked Risks
- Security incidents

# Stakeholders

- Legal Department
- Compliance Officers

# Contextual Constraints

- Highly regulated industries


# Detection Source
Compliance Reports

# Standardized Risk Entry
Legacy modernization initiatives may fail to meet modern compliance and governance requirements.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "Failing to modernize can result in non-compliance, leading to reputational damage and financial penalties."

# Logic Pattern
IF modernization does not address regulatory requirements THEN compliance risk increases
