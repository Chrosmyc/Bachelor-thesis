# Description
Insufficient communication and change management may reduce stakeholder buy-in, alignment, expectation management, and smooth workflow or role transitions.

# Root Cause
Incremental modernization changes processes, workflows, roles, and benefit-realization timelines.

# Trigger Event
The project proceeds without clear communication, aligned expectations, or structured change-management practices.

# Impact
Low buy-in, misalignment, confusion, weak adoption, transition friction, and missed learning opportunities.

# Linked Risks
- R139; R152; R146

# Stakeholders

- Stakeholders; users; business owners; development teams; operations

# Contextual Constraints

- Modernization initiatives with organizational process changes and incremental benefit realization.


# Detection Source
Stakeholder Communication / Interview

# Standardized Risk Entry
If communication and change management are weak during incremental modernization, stakeholders may be misaligned and transitions in workflows or roles may become difficult.
