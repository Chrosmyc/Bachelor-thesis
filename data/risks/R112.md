# Description
AI systems used in data-intensive enterprise environments may propagate sensitive information through downstream systems without adequate audit trails.

# Root Cause
Privacy and ethical exposure from unvalidated AI systems and insufficient auditability.

# Trigger Event
AI systems are deployed or used in data-intensive modernization workflows without adequate audit trails.

# Impact
Sensitive information may propagate downstream, creating privacy, ethical, and governance exposure.

# Linked Risks
- R111
- R113

# Stakeholders

- Security teams
- governance teams
- compliance functions
- data owners
- affected users

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies to data-intensive enterprise environments using AI-assisted automation.


# Detection Source
Operational Data / Project Plan

# Standardized Risk Entry
AI-enabled modernization can create privacy and ethical risk when AI outputs move through downstream systems without adequate audit trails. This is especially relevant in data-intensive enterprise environments.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "This risk is compounded by the privacy and ethical exposure that AI systems create when deployed in data-intensive enterprise environments, where unvalidated AI outputs can propagate sensitive information through downstream systems without adequate audit trails."

# Logic Pattern
IF AI systems process sensitive enterprise data AND audit trails are inadequate THEN sensitive information may propagate downstream.
