# Description
Modernization starts based on an IT perspective instead of a business perspective, leading to results that do not support primary business goals.

# Root Cause
Focusing only on technical implementation without considering business strategy.

# Trigger Event
Starting the project from the IT department without executive business involvement.

# Impact
No return on investment (ROI); IT systems grow in cost without business value.

# Linked Risks
- Budget Overrun
- Project Failure

# Stakeholders

- Business Executives
- IT Managers
- Project Managers

# Contextual Constraints



# Detection Source
Project Plan / Strategy Documents

# Standardized Risk Entry
Technical focus without business alignment risks a lack of ROI and failure to meet organizational goals.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "The implementation might appear successful at that time but the impact after adopting the new architecture could be not aligned with the business goals of the company."

# Logic Pattern
IF [Only IT Perspective is followed] AND [SOA Adoption Starts] THEN [Business Misalignment occurs]
