# Description
Relevant modernization knowledge may be missed or inconsistently classified because terms related to software modernization are not well defined.

# Root Cause
Modernization terms and contributions are not consolidated, and related terms are wide and diverse.

# Trigger Event
Researchers or practitioners search, classify, or describe modernization scenarios using narrow or inconsistent terminology.

# Impact
Relevant modernization approaches, risks, or mitigations may be omitted from analysis or reuse.

# Linked Risks
- R60

# Stakeholders

- Researchers; architects; project managers

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies to literature-based modernization analysis and scenario characterization.


# Detection Source
Project Plan

# Standardized Risk Entry
If modernization terminology is inconsistent, relevant modernization knowledge may be missed or classified inconsistently during analysis and reuse.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "the terms related to software modernization have not been well defined yet"

# Logic Pattern
IF modernization terminology is not well defined AND knowledge is extracted through search or classification THEN relevant knowledge may be missed or inconsistently structured.
