# Description
Modernization may lack sufficient business sponsorship or funding.

# Root Cause
Difficulty getting money and business sponsor support.

# Trigger Event
Modernization requires budget approval or continued financing.

# Impact
Project delay, cancellation, reduced scope.

# Linked Risks

# Stakeholders

- C-level management
- sponsors
- project managers

# Contextual Constraints



# Detection Source
Business Case / Project Plan

# Standardized Risk Entry
Modernization may lack sufficient business sponsorship or funding.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "Difficult to get money to support the legacy modernization project."

# Logic Pattern
IF modernization funding is not secured AND project execution depends on business sponsorship THEN the modernization project may be delayed or stopped.
