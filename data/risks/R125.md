# Description
Migration decisions are evaluated by technical completion rather than business outcome achievement. This creates a risk that the selected strategy does not actually satisfy the business driver.

# Root Cause
Missing success metrics that validate business outcomes.

# Trigger Event
The organization completes migration or modernization work without measurable outcome validation.

# Impact
Business driver may not be achieved, value realization remains unclear, and technical completion may hide business failure.

# Linked Risks
- R124
- R132
- R138

# Stakeholders

- Business owners
- executive stakeholders
- cloud strategy team
- technical leads.

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies when success metrics are not defined for the chosen migration strategy.


# Detection Source
Project Plan / success metrics / business case workshop

# Standardized Risk Entry
Without success metrics, a migration may be considered complete technically while failing to validate business value.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> [Paraphrased evidence from G10] Success metrics confirm whether the business driver was achieved.

# Logic Pattern
IF success metrics are absent AND strategy outcomes are judged technically THEN business-value failure may remain undetected.
