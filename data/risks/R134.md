# Description
If mitigation actions, responsible parties, resolution timelines, and status are not documented, identified risks may not be actively managed or resolved.

# Root Cause
Mitigation strategies are not defined or tracked.

# Trigger Event
Risks are identified but mitigation actions, owners, timelines, and status are not maintained.

# Impact
Risks remain unresolved, responsibilities are unclear, timelines slip, blockers persist.

# Linked Risks

# Stakeholders

- Migration team
- governance team
- risk owners
- workload owners

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies after risks have been identified.


# Detection Source
Project Plan

# Standardized Risk Entry
Identified modernization risks can remain unresolved if mitigation actions, owners, timelines, and status are not tracked.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "document mitigation actions, responsible parties, and resolution timelines"

# Logic Pattern
IF risks are identified AND mitigation ownership/status is not tracked THEN risks may remain unresolved.
