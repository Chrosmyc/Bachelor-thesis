# Description
Organizations that aggressively automate systems without preserving institutional knowledge may unintentionally increase operational risk.

# Root Cause
Institutional knowledge is not preserved during automation and transition

# Trigger Event
Aggressive automation without knowledge management, documentation, or transfer planning

# Impact
Increased operational risk; weakened continuity during modernization

# Linked Risks
- R41

# Stakeholders

- Technical teams; senior employees; operational teams; leadership

# Contextual Constraints

- Legacy-driven environments where institutional knowledge is concentrated and documentation may be incomplete


# Detection Source
Project Plan / Interview / Operational Data

# Standardized Risk Entry
Aggressive automation can increase operational risk if institutional knowledge is not preserved before or during the modernization transition.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "Organizations that aggressively automate systems without preserving institutional knowledge may unintentionally increase operational risk."

# Logic Pattern
IF automation is introduced aggressively AND institutional knowledge is not preserved THEN operational risk may increase.
