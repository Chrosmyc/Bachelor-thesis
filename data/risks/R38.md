# Description
Abrupt replacement of critical systems can increase operational disruption because critical systems require gradual evolution and validation during transition stages.

# Root Cause
Critical systems are modernized too abruptly instead of gradually

# Trigger Event
Abrupt replacement of critical systems

# Impact
Operational disruption; insufficient reliability validation during transition

# Linked Risks

# Stakeholders

- Technical teams; operational teams; business units; leadership

# Contextual Constraints

- Critical systems where reliability and continuity are essential


# Detection Source
Project Plan / Architecture Diagram / Operational Data

# Standardized Risk Entry
Abrupt replacement of critical systems can disrupt operations if reliability is not validated through gradual transition stages.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "Critical systems should evolve gradually rather than through abrupt replacement."

# Logic Pattern
IF critical systems are replaced abruptly AND transition reliability is not validated THEN operational disruption may occur.
