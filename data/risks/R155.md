# Description
Delaying modernization can keep financial resources tied to maintaining outdated systems instead of using them for innovation, growth, and digital transformation.

# Root Cause
High technical-debt and maintenance spending; budget constraints; postponement of modernization

# Trigger Event
Management decides to postpone modernization or continues funding legacy maintenance over modernization

# Impact
Reduced profits, reduced growth potential, missed market opportunities, and falling behind more agile competitors

# Linked Risks
- R156

# Stakeholders

- COO; executive leadership; IT leadership; finance; business units

# Contextual Constraints

- Organizations with legacy applications and substantial IT spending on maintenance or technical debt


# Detection Source
Project Plan; Operational Data

# Standardized Risk Entry
If modernization is postponed and IT spending remains locked into legacy maintenance, the organization may lose profit and growth opportunities while competitors gain agility.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "The longer you wait to modernize, the more you risk falling behind competitors who are agile and ready to seize market opportunities."

# Logic Pattern
IF modernization is delayed AND IT budget remains consumed by legacy maintenance THEN forgone profits and competitive disadvantage may occur.
