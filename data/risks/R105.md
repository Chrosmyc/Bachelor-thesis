# Description
Chargeback funding can jeopardize modernization when users are unwilling or unable to pay their share of costs.

# Root Cause
Costs are spread across a benefiting user community that may not accept or afford them.

# Trigger Event
Agency uses a chargeback model to finance modernization.

# Impact
Funding shortfall, jeopardized modernization effort, additional overhead.

# Linked Risks
- R100

# Stakeholders

- User community; finance; agency leadership; program manager

# Contextual Constraints

- Service provision or shared funding models.


# Detection Source
Project Plan

# Standardized Risk Entry
Modernization financing may fail when chargeback users do not pay their allocated costs.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "users may be unwilling or unable to pay their share of the costs, jeopardizing the modernization effort"

# Logic Pattern
IF modernization is financed through chargeback AND users cannot or will not pay THEN the modernization effort may be jeopardized.
