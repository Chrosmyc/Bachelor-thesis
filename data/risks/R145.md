# Description
After modernization reaches production, the modernized solution may not realize the value stakeholders expected if performance is not monitored, measured, reported, supported, and updated.

# Root Cause
Insufficient production monitoring, measuring, support, update, and stakeholder reporting.

# Trigger Event
Modernized solution enters production without ongoing measurement against planned objectives.

# Impact
Stakeholder expectations may not be validated, business value may remain unproven, and improvement needs may be missed.

# Linked Risks
- R143
- R146

# Stakeholders

- Stakeholders
- agency leadership
- production support teams
- business users
- technology teams

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies during Production stage after transformation.


# Detection Source
Operational Data

# Standardized Risk Entry
If the modernized environment is not monitored and measured in production, the agency may not know whether modernization achieved planned stakeholder value.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "measure the performance of the environment"

# Logic Pattern
IF modernization enters production AND performance/objectives are not measured THEN stakeholder value realization risk increases.
