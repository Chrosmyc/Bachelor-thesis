# Description
Stakeholder needs that are not anticipated during a full rewrite can derail modernization and create transition problems.

# Root Cause
Unanticipated stakeholder needs.

# Trigger Event
Full rewrite planning or transition without sufficiently understanding stakeholder and user needs.

# Impact
Rewrite derailment, failed transition, user frustration, reduced transition effectiveness.

# Linked Risks

# Stakeholders

- Users; citizens; internal teams; product owners; stakeholders

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies when modernization affects public-service users and internal operations.


# Detection Source
Interview

# Standardized Risk Entry
Modernization may fail or lose effectiveness when unanticipated stakeholder needs emerge during a full rewrite.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "unanticipated stakeholder needs can derail even the most well-intentioned full rewrites."

# Logic Pattern
IF stakeholder needs are not understood AND a full rewrite is executed THEN the modernization may be derailed or poorly adopted.
