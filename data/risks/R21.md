# Description
Modernization activities may interrupt mission-critical services and business operations.

# Root Cause
Large-scale infrastructure transition

# Trigger Event
System migration or deployment activities

# Impact
Service disruption, operational interruption, financial losses

# Linked Risks
- Business continuity failure

# Stakeholders

- Project Manager
- IT Operations

# Contextual Constraints

- Mission-critical systems


# Detection Source
Monitoring Systems

# Standardized Risk Entry
Modernization projects may introduce downtime and operational disruption during migration.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "Downtime can result in significant financial and operational losses."

# Logic Pattern
IF critical systems are migrated without controlled transition THEN downtime risk increases
