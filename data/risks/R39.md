# Description
Organizations may create an “old versus new” technology culture where institutional experience and modern technical expertise compete rather than collaborate.

# Root Cause
Generational technology gap and competing perceptions of legacy environments versus rapid automation

# Trigger Event
New modernization-oriented teams work alongside senior personnel without collaborative governance

# Impact
Slowed transformation, reduced collaboration, cultural tension, reduced modernization effectiveness

# Linked Risks
- R34
- R35
- R38

# Stakeholders

- Senior personnel; newer technical teams; leadership; operational teams

# Contextual Constraints

- Organizations with both experienced legacy personnel and newer cloud/AI/automation specialists


# Detection Source
Interview / Project Plan

# Standardized Risk Entry
Modernization may slow when organizations frame legacy expertise and modern technical expertise as competing groups rather than complementary capabilities.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "Organizations that create an “old versus new” technology culture often slow their own transformation efforts."

# Logic Pattern
IF senior personnel and newer technical teams are positioned as competitors THEN modernization collaboration may decrease.
