# Description
Modernization initiatives may face hidden resistance such as delayed adoption, reduced cooperation, limited knowledge sharing, distrust toward newer technologies, and isolation of newly hired technical personnel.

# Root Cause
Organizational resistance hidden beneath modernization efforts

# Trigger Event
Heavy investment in automation platforms, analytics systems, ETL pipelines, cloud migration, or AI-driven operations

# Impact
Modernization slows before technology becomes the primary problem; newer technical employees lose influence in operational decisions

# Linked Risks
- R34
- R36
- R37
- R38

# Stakeholders

- Operational teams; newly hired technical personnel; leadership

# Contextual Constraints

- Legacy-driven environments with long-established operational teams and authority structures


# Detection Source
Project Plan / Interview / Operational Data

# Standardized Risk Entry
Modernization may be undermined by silent organizational resistance that appears as delayed adoption, reduced cooperation, limited knowledge sharing, and distrust toward new technologies.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "As a result, modernization initiatives may face silent resistance: delayed adoption, reduced cooperation, limited knowledge sharing, distrust toward newer technologies, isolation of newly hired technical personnel."

# Logic Pattern
IF modernization changes operational authority structures AND resistance remains unmanaged THEN delayed adoption and reduced cooperation may occur.
