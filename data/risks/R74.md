# Description
An approach validated on small benchmark scenarios may not scale directly to production legacy systems that are orders of magnitude larger.

# Root Cause
Large codebases require chunking, hierarchical specifications, and incremental validation not yet implemented in the evaluated framework.

# Trigger Event
The method is applied to real systems much larger than the evaluated 100–310 LOC scenarios.

# Impact
Analysis may become incomplete, BSGs may become unmanageable, and validation may become too expensive or slow.

# Linked Risks

# Stakeholders

- Architects
- modernization teams
- project managers
- QA teams

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies when moving from benchmark scenarios to production-scale modernization.


# Detection Source
Project Plan, Source Code, BSG artifacts, Test Suite

# Standardized Risk Entry
If an LLM-based BSG pipeline is applied to production-scale systems without scaling mechanisms, modernization quality and feasibility may degrade.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "Real legacy systems are orders of magnitude larger. Scaling AgentModernize would require chunking strategies for the Legacy Analyzer, hierarchical BSGs, and incremental validation."

# Logic Pattern
IF codebase size greatly exceeds evaluated scale AND no chunking or hierarchical BSG is used THEN modernization process scalability risk increases
