# Description
Different driving forces, needs, constraints, available resources, and expectations may conflict during modernization, making decision-making and prioritization uncertain.

# Root Cause
Each company has its own scenario and multiple driving forces can conflict.

# Trigger Event
Stakeholders pursue several modernization benefits such as scalability, deployment, maintainability, agility, and time-to-market without explicit trade-off analysis.

# Impact
Misaligned priorities; weak feasibility decision; increased chance of unsuccessful modernization or resource waste.

# Linked Risks
- R81
- R83

# Stakeholders

- Managers; engineers; developers; customers

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies during initiation when companies decide whether modernization with microservices is feasible.


# Detection Source
Project Plan / Interview

# Standardized Risk Entry
If modernization drivers conflict and are not analyzed as trade-offs, the modernization decision may become misaligned and risky.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> As conflicts can emerge among those driving forces, a trade-off analysis could be performed by the stakeholders.

# Logic Pattern
IF multiple driving forces conflict AND no trade-off analysis is performed THEN modernization priorities may become misaligned.
