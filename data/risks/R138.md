# Description
Slices that are too simple or too low in business value may provide limited learning, limited business impact, or may not justify the replacement effort.

# Root Cause
Slice selection may prioritize ease over meaningful learning or value delivery.

# Trigger Event
The team selects a migration slice with too little complexity or too little business value.

# Impact
Low stakeholder value, weak evidence of modernization benefit, wasted effort, and reduced learning for later slices.

# Linked Risks
- R152; R151

# Stakeholders

- Modernization team; business stakeholders; management

# Contextual Constraints

- Early slice selection in Strangler Fig modernization.


# Detection Source
Project Plan / Backlog

# Standardized Risk Entry
If selected modernization slices are too simple or low-value, the project may produce little learning or business impact relative to the effort.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> 

# Logic Pattern
IF a slice is too simple or low-value AND it is selected for migration THEN learning/value risk may materialize.
