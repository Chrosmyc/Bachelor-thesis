# Description
Unexpected interdependencies can derail a full rewrite because hidden dependencies may only become visible during modernization.

# Root Cause
Unexpected interdependencies across system parts.

# Trigger Event
Large-scale full rewrite or migration exposes hidden dependencies.

# Impact
Modernization derailment, transition failure, increased complexity, and potential service disruption.

# Linked Risks

# Stakeholders

- Architecture team; development team; product owners; operations teams

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies to large-scale government projects and tightly integrated systems.


# Detection Source
Architecture Diagram

# Standardized Risk Entry
Full rewrites can be derailed when unexpected interdependencies are discovered during modernization.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "unexpected interdependencies, missing documentation, and unanticipated stakeholder needs can derail even the most well-intentioned full rewrites."

# Logic Pattern
IF a full rewrite is attempted AND unexpected interdependencies exist THEN the rewrite may be derailed.
