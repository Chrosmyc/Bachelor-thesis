# Description
Modernization work packages may be ordered poorly if priorities are not assessed using time, money, risk, reward, dependencies, complexity, and stakeholder requirements.

# Root Cause
Lack of systematic capability prioritization and portfolio analysis.

# Trigger Event
Roadmap components are selected or sequenced without structured prioritization.

# Impact
Lower-value work may be executed first, high-risk dependencies may be missed, budget may be used inefficiently, and transformation readiness may be weak.

# Linked Risks
- R143
- R145

- R152

# Stakeholders

- Agency leadership
- business teams
- technology teams
- portfolio managers
- funding stakeholders

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies when multiple modernization capabilities
- work packages
- or migration projects compete for limited resources.


# Detection Source
Project Plan

# Standardized Risk Entry
If modernization components are not prioritized systematically, the roadmap may sequence work in a way that increases risk, cost, or dependency problems.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "Time, Money, Risk, Reward"

# Logic Pattern
IF multiple modernization components compete for resources AND priority is not systematically assessed THEN roadmap misprioritization risk increases.
