# Description
Modernization may fail to gain acceptance when reasons and consequences are not communicated effectively.

# Root Cause
Difficulty making involved people accept modernization and its consequences.

# Trigger Event
Modernization affects people who do not understand why change is needed.

# Impact
Reduced acceptance, confusion, opposition to the new system.

# Linked Risks

# Stakeholders

- Business users
- IT staff
- managers

# Contextual Constraints



# Detection Source
Interview / Communication Plan

# Standardized Risk Entry
Modernization may fail to gain acceptance when reasons and consequences are not communicated effectively.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "Difficult to make people, who involve in legacy modernization, to accept the legacy modernization together with its consequences."

# Logic Pattern
IF reasons and consequences are not communicated AND people are affected by modernization THEN acceptance risk increases.
