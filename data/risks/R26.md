# Description
Selecting replacement components that are incompatible with later modernization increments may require additional development increments and delay modernization schedules.

# Root Cause
Early technology decisions are made without sufficient alignment to future modernization architecture.

# Trigger Event
Selection of incompatible middleware or replacement technologies during Increment 1.

# Impact
Increased development costs, additional modernization increments, delayed schedules.

# Linked Risks
- Cost Overrun
- Architecture Misalignment

# Stakeholders

- Software Architect
- Project Manager

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies when modernization occurs incrementally across multiple phases.


# Detection Source
Architecture Diagram

# Standardized Risk Entry
Early selection of incompatible modernization components can delay later modernization phases and increase redevelopment effort.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "Using the wrong product may require an additional development increment between Increment 1 and Increment 2."

# Logic Pattern
IF incompatible components are selected during early migration AND future modernization requirements are not considered THEN schedule and cost risks increase.
