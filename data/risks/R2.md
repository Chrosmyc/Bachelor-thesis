# Description
Loss of knowledge as original developers retire or leave, combined with outdated or missing system documentation.

# Root Cause
Aging workforce and lack of continuous documentation updates during the system's lifecycle

# Trigger Event
Retirement of key personnel

# Impact
Project delays, inability to understand business logic, increased risk of system failure during modernization.

# Linked Risks
- Incomplete or Erroneous Data Migration
- Time Overrun

# Stakeholders

- IT Manager
- HR
- Lead Architect

# Contextual Constraints

- Leaving of a former architect


# Detection Source
Documentation Audit

# Standardized Risk Entry
The loss of institutional knowledge and lack of up-to-date documentation regarding legacy system internals significantly increase the risk of errors and project failure during modernization

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "The big problem is that you can't find people to understand them (legacy systems) and understand the technology"

# Logic Pattern
IF [Original Developers Absent] AND [Documentation Outdated] THEN [Scarcity of Experts and Documentation]
