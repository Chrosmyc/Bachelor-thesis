# Description
Modernization may be affected if third-party tools, agreements, statutory requirements, or third-party items are not identified and verified.

# Root Cause
Unclear third-party dependencies, agreements, or statutory requirements.

# Trigger Event
Project relies on third-party items without explicit identification, agreement, and functionality verification.

# Impact
Supplier-related issues or third-party functionality problems during modernization.

# Linked Risks
- R52
- R58

# Stakeholders

- Suppliers; project managers; users; QA team

# Contextual Constraints

- Applies when external suppliers or third-party tools/items are part of modernization.


# Detection Source
Project Plan; Supplier agreements; Test results

# Standardized Risk Entry
If third-party tools, agreements, statutory requirements, or supplier items are not managed and verified, modernization may face supplier or third-party functionality risks.

# Key Quote for Prompting
> "Identify third party tools, agreements and other statutory requirements"

# Logic Pattern
IF third-party dependencies are not identified and verified THEN supplier or third-party functionality problems may occur.
